Identifying Real versus AI Art

Meta announced that they will be tagging posts that include AI generated content. I thought this was a fun project to emulate and a I learned about how to deal with data processing with Python, including file conversions and data cleaning. This project aims to identify AI generated art versus real (web scraped) art. Art has always been a reflection of human creativity, emotion, and expression. With the advent of AI, machines have started to create art that closely mimics human styles. The goal of this project is to develop a model that can accurately differentiate between real art and AI-generated art. This not only tests the capabilities of AI but also explores the boundaries of human perception and machine learning.
